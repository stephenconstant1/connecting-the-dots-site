What CMI Does

Five Systems, One Client at a Time.

Financial Architecture

Custom budgeting, debt-reduction, and savings frameworks built around each client's real numbers.

Investment Planning

Rules-based portfolio construction — position sizing, risk limits, and disciplined entries and exits.

Business Development

Cash-flow systems, workflow design, and business plans for owners who need structure, not more meetings.

Financial Education

Workshops and youth financial-literacy programs delivered directly to schools and community organizations.

The Pot Investment Fund

A tiered, income-generating fund in development — built on a diversified portfolio with a downside-protection buffer.
