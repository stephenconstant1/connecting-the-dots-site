Our Mission

"Provide clarity, consistency, and compounding through tailored strategies that help clients build lasting wealth."

CMI exists because most families and small businesses are priced out of real financial strategy — not because they don't need it, but because the advisors who provide it are built for someone else's balance sheet. CMI closes that gap: institutional-style discipline, delivered at a scale and price built for everyday people.

— Stephen Constant, Founder & Financial Architect
What We Do

Structured systems, not one-off advice.

Every engagement is built to leave the client with a repeatable system — something they can run themselves, long after the conversation ends.

Plan

Financial Architecture

Custom budgeting, debt-reduction, and savings frameworks built around each client's real numbers.

Invest

Investment Planning

Rules-based portfolio construction — position sizing, risk limits, and disciplined entries and exits.

Build

Teach

Workshops and youth financial-literacy programs delivered directly to schools and community organizations.
